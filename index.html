Joshua Hou

Joshua Hou is an accordionist and keyboardist based in Seattle, Washington. He can be found singing and playing various kinds of music in a variety of venues in the greater Seattle area.

He can be contacted at me@joshuahou.com

Projects

  • How Short
    https://howshortjazz.com

    Happy swing with sweet harmonies with Andy Short (guitar/vocals) and Josh Hou (accordion/vocals)

  • Key Up

    Instrumental jazz with trumpet, accordion and bass.

  • Jazztalk Seattle Podcast
    https://soundcloud.com/jazztalksea
