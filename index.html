Joshua Hou

Joshua Hou is a jazz pianist/accordionist based in Seattle, Washington. He can be found singing and playing music in a variety of venues in the greater Seattle area.

He can be contacted at me@joshuahou.com

Projects

  • How Short

    Happy swing with sweet harmonies with Andy Short (guitar/vox) and Josh Hou (accordion/vox)

  • Key Up

    Instrumental jazz with Ray Larsen (trumpet), Abbey Blackwell (bass), Josh Hou (accordion)

  • Jazztalk Seattle Podcast

    Max Holmberg (drums, percussion) and Josh Hou (accordion, keyboards) talk about jazz: records they're listening to, music they're working on, and stuff to check out in Seattle.
