<b>小白船</b><br/>
<br/>
藍藍的天空銀河裏<br/>
有只小白船<br/>
船上有棵桂花樹<br/>
白兔在遊玩<br/>
<br/>
槳兒槳兒看不見<br/>
船上也沒帆<br/>
飄呀飄呀飄向西天<br/>
<br/>
渡過那條銀河水<br/>
走向雲彩國<br/>
走過那個雲彩國<br/>
再向哪兒去?<br/>
<br/>
在那遙遠的地方<br/>
閃著金光<br/>
晨星是燈塔<br/>
照呀照得亮<br/>

<b>小白船 Little White Boat</b><br/>
<br/>
In the blue sky, in the milky way<br/>
There is a little white boat<br/>
On the boat is an Osmanthus tree<br/>
A white rabbit is playing<br/>
<br/>
Oars, oars? Cannot be seen!<br/>
Nor a sail on the boat<br/>
Drifting, drifting<br/>
Drifting towards the western sky<br/>
<br/>
Crossing over that silver river<br/>
Towards the land of clouds<br/>
After passing the land of clouds<br/>
Where to go next?<br/>
<br/>
At that faraway place<br/>
Golden light sparkles<br/>
The morning star is a lighthouse<br/>
Shines, oh, shines so bright<br/>
